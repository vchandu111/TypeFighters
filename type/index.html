Typing Game
Instructions:

  • You need to write the sentences in the text area.
  • You will have three levels in this game
    Easy : Time span is 35 seconds
    Medium : Time span is 20 seconds
    Hard : Time span is 15 seconds
  • To get next sentence you need to type the current sentence correctly without error.
  • You will be provided speed for every particular right sentence that you finish typing.

Happy Typing!

Word Game
Instructions:

  • You will have 7 sec as initial time and it will get updated based on game levels
    Easy : Increases time by 5 secs on typing the word correctly
    Medium : Increases time by 3 secs on typing the word correctly
    Hard : Increases time by 2 secs on typing the word correctly
  • You will lose 1 point for every word you type wrong.
  • Your Final Score and Accuracy will be calculated based on right and wrong responses
